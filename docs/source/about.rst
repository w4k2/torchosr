########
About us
########

The ``torchosr`` package was created for the needs of the `Department of Systems and Computer Networks <http://kssk.pwr.edu.pl>`_, *Wroc≈Çaw University of Science and Technology*, as part of research projects regarding Open Set Recognition and its code is used for experimental evaluation since 2022.

The main author and maintainer of its current version is the employee of the unit, namely `J. Komorniczak <https://www.kssk.pwr.edu.pl/komorniczak>`_.
